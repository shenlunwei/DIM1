Dim to warm LED is the forefront technology. Featuring an intelligent circuit, Dim-to-Warm Tape automatically changes Kelvin temp. from warm white 3000K to very warm white 1800K, simply by dimming the LED light. Using a normal dimmer with 0/1-10V low voltage Volt dimmable drivers, smoothly warm or cool the color temperature of tape light to suit the mood.

Because it features an intelligent circuit design that automatically adjusts the color temperature, warm-dim lighting does not require any extra accessories like controllers or receivers. This saves costs and means it’s very easy to use, with no batteries or controllers to keep track of.

  • The light warms as it is dimmed
  • Dim to Warm white using PWM dimming
  • Double sided TESA 4965 tape backing, UL recognised PCBS
  • Can be cut to size at marked intervals
  • No batteries, controllers, or receivers required
  • Intelligent circuit eliminates the need for accessories and saves costs
  • 90 CRI (Color Rendering Index)
